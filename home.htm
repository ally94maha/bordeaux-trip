HOME

Bordeaux is a port city on the Garonne River in southwestern France. Celebrating wine, gastronomy, arts and culture, it's the ultimate city for those who enjoy the good life. Bordeaux is typical and modern at the same time, with its broad pedestrian boulevards, gorgeous squares and a mix of modern buildings and historical architecture. It represents the very essence of the French spirit. Since 2007, almost all of Bordeaux city centre has been listed as a Unesco World Heritage site.
